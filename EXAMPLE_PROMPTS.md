# Example Prompts for the Proposal Workflow

Use these prompts in the **Proposal Agent** to automatically create contacts, schedule meetings, and generate personalized emails. The system will:
1. Extract recipient name and email
2. Identify their role (Financial, Legal, Indigenous, Environmental, Community)
3. Extract the specific purpose/context from your message
4. Generate a role-specific email that mentions that exact purpose
5. Create a meeting invitation if requested

## ‚ú® Pro Tips

### Context Matters
- **Specific:** "add Financial Advisor for budget planning" ‚Üí Email about budgets
- **Generic:** "add Financial Advisor" ‚Üí Email about general project needs

### Try Different Phrasings
All of these work:
- "for [purpose]"
- "regarding [context]"
- "about [topic]"
- "on [subject]"

### Examples
```
schedule meeting with Lawyer for contract negotiations
schedule meeting with Lawyer regarding land regulations
schedule meeting with Lawyer about indigenous rights
schedule meeting with Lawyer on environmental compliance
```

All work and generate appropriate emails!

### Mix Roles and Purposes
The system intelligently detects roles and contexts:
```
add Financial Expert at finance@company.com for sustainability fund development
```
‚Üí Email emphasizes both financial AND sustainability aspects

```
add Community Member at resident@neighborhood.org for environmental restoration
```
‚Üí Email focuses on both community participation AND ecological benefits
